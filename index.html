ABOUT US

ITER was founded in 1849 by Prof. (Dr.) Manojranjan Nayak who believed in the importance of a quality education. We have a long history of academic excellence, and our graduates have gone on to successful careers in a variety of fields. Our campus is located in a beautiful setting, and we offer a variety of student services and activities. We are committed to providing our students with a well-rounded education that will prepare them for success in their careers and lives.
